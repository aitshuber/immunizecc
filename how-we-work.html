How We Work

TIP promotes a community-based pro-vaccine movement across the state, ignited by the expertise of our staff and advisors, cultivated through faith-based and community leaders, and disseminated through local vaccine champions. We engage and activate pro-vaccine influencers in key communities to inspire fact-based vaccine decision-making through trusted sources. Our work is focused on inclusivity and equity for all Texans.

Our Partners

We are the trusted resource for increasing immunization rates to create healthier communities and eradicate vaccine-preventable diseases. Our team includes public health and public policy experts plus leaders in medicine, both practitioners and researchers, as well as talented leaders in communications, education and health care administration.
