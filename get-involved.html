Internship Opportunities

The internship opportunities at TIP allow students to explore the ins and outs of immunization advocacy and education. These opportunities are open to a wide range of students:

  • Recent high school graduates entering college

  • Undergraduate students

  • Graduate students (e.g., J.D., M.B.A., M.D., M.P.H., Ph.D.)

Guest Speaker

TIP organizes and hosts impactful events that can only be successful with the assistance of those who dedicate their time and efforts to our mission. We need speakers at a variety of events including:

  • Our annual Community Immunity Luncheon

  • Educational webinars

  • In-person forums (e.g., School Nurse Forum)

  • Conferences (e.g., Texas Immunization Conference)
