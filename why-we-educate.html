We Educate

The Immunization Partnership tailors the education and training needs of local communities and organizations to increase vaccine confidence and understand barriers to immunizations. TIP's education is developed from the latest information, best practices and resources to offer solution-based strategies within diverse communities.

Immunization University

Immunization University (IZU) is an online educational platform providing courses on various immunization topics. Some courses offer continuing education credit for nurses through a partnership with Cizik School of Nursing at UTHealth Houston.

Building Coalitions Across Texas 

Building Coalitions Across Texas (BCAT) connects immunization coalitions across the state. These Coalitions are instrumental in promoting and raising immunization rates at the local level, helping keep all Texans protected from outbreaks of vaccine-preventable diseases.

Immunization Trainings

The Immunization Partnership equips community partners such as school districts, colleges and universities with information and resources they need to advance vaccine confidence.
