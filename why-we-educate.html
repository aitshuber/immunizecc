We Educate

The Immunization Partnership tailors the education and training needs of local communities and organizations to increase vaccine confidence and understand barriers to immunizations. TIP's education is developed from the latest information, best practices and resources to offer solution-based strategies within diverse communities.

Immunization University

IZU is an online educational platform providing courses on various immunization topics. Some courses offer continuing education credit for nurses through a partnership with Cizik School of Nursing at UTHealth Houston.

Building Coalitions across Texas (BCaT)

Building Coalitions across Texas (BCaT) connects immunization coalitions across Texas. Coalitions are instrumental in promoting and raising immunization rates at the local level, which helps keep all of Texas protected from outbreaks of vaccine-preventable diseases.

Immunization Trainings

The Immunization Partnership engages with school districts, colleges and universities, and community organizations to advance vaccine confidence through immunization trainings and workshops.
